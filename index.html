My Work

After graduating from the University of Michigan in May 2019 with a BS in computer science, I took a role as an SDE I at Amazon Web Services. I'm currently based in Portland, Oregon, and am enjoying the beautiful nature of the Pacific Northwest. In my current role on the GlobalAccelerator team, I play a part in all of our customer facing APIs. After starting at Amazon, I was given the opportunity to lead our service's integration with resource tagging, a feature shared across service teams that brings direct value to customers. In fact, almost all of the work I do is with the purpose of creating customer value. Whether that's being a part of making CloudWatch metrics available, providing fine grained resource management with tagging, being in on customer calls, or developing valuable tests that have caught bugs helping to keep customer trust.

My Goals

I’m an extremely driven person, I enjoy learning new things, and am happiest when I am being challenged or being creative. I’m someone who loves the space of computer science, and my goal is to continue to learn as much as I can and hopefully get to the point where I lead my own teams. My next big goal is to make SDEII.
